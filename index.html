About SAN

Study Aboard Network

Study Abroad Network is a premier study abroad consultancy, focused on offering comprehensive services and expert support to students who are looking to pursue their academic aspirations overseas. Our team of committed education specialists is passionate about assisting students in realizing their potential and achieving their dreams.

We recognize that the process of studying abroad can be overwhelming, which is why we offer personalized advice and unwavering support to ensure that every student has a smooth experience. We provide the most recent information about courses, scholarships, and more through our broad network of connections with leading international educational institutions, ensuring that every student has access to the greatest options. At SAN, we're committed to giving students the tools they need to succeed in their study abroad programs.

Services at SAN
Your Gateway to Global Education

Post-Arrival Support

Experience as comfortable

Our post-arrival support includes assistance with accommodation, bank account setup, registration, and adapting to the new culture. We're here to make your study abroad experience as comfortable as possible

EXPERT COUNSELING

consultants will help you

Our overseas education consultants will help you identify your goals, analyze your academic background, and assess your career aspirations to guide you in choosing the right course and university for your study abroad journey.

Application Assistance

preparing essential documents

Our overseas education consultants meticulously guide you through preparing essential documents, completing forms, and submitting applications to your preferred universities.

Visa Guidance

prepare your visa application

Navigating the visa process can be daunting, but our team is here to guide you through every step. We'll help you prepare your visa application, gather the necessary documents, and guide you in preparing for the visa interview

Scholarship Assistance

apply for scholarships

We understand that financing your education abroad can be challenging. Our team will help you explore and apply for scholarships, grants, and financial aid options that are available to you.

Pre-Departure Briefing

smooth transition

Get ready for your study abroad journey with our pre-departure briefing. We'll provide you with essential information on your stay, travel arrangements, and more to ensure a smooth transition to your new environment.
